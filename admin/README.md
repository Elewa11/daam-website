# 📖 Daam Website: Admin & Editing Guide

This guide explains how to use the **Admin Panel** to manage and update your website content live.

## 🚀 1. Accessing the Admin Panel
- **Admin Link**: [https://elewa11.github.io/daam-website/admin/](https://elewa11.github.io/daam-website/admin/)
- **Password**: `admin123`
- **Auto-Login**: Once you log in, the system will remember you. You won't need to log in again on the same browser unless you log out.

---

## 🛠️ 2. The Editor Interface

At the top of the page, you will see the **Admin Control Bar**:
- **تصفح (Browse Mode)**: Navigate the website normally like a visitor.
- **تعديل (Edit Mode)**: Click on any text or image to change it.
- **نشر التعديلات (Publish)**: Saves all your changes to the live site.

---

## 📝 3. Editing Content

### Text Editing & Formatting
While in **Edit Mode**, click on any text element. A **Rich Text Toolbar** will appear at the top:
1. **Selection**: Highlight the text you want to change.
2. **Colors (Left)**: Use the squares to change **Text Color** (A) or **Highlight Color** (🎨).
3. **Font Size**: Change text size from "صغير جداً" to "ضخم".
4. **Alignment**: Align text to the Right, Center, or Left.
5. **Styling (Right)**: Apply **Bold (B)**, *Italic (/)*, or <u>Underline (U)</u>.

### Image Replacement
- **Standard Images**: Click any image to open the upload window.
- **Card Placeholders**: Click the gray boxes or card areas to upload a background image.
- **Confirm**: After choosing a file, click "تأكيد واستبدال" to apply it to the page.

### Deleting Sections
- Hover over any section (Hero, Programs, Team, etc.).
- Click the **Red Trash Icon (🗑)** that appears in the top corner.
- **Confirm**: Click OK to hide the section.

---

## ☁️ 4. Saving & Publishing

### The Change Counter
Next to the Publish button, you will see a counter (e.g., `3 تعديلات`). This tracks:
- Text changes
- New images uploaded
- Deleted sections

### Publishing to Live
1. Click **"نشر التعديلات"**.
2. Wait for the success message ("تم النشر بنجاح").
3. **Crucial Note**: After publishing, GitHub takes **1 to 2 minutes** to update the live site. You can check the progress [here](https://github.com/Elewa11/daam-website/actions).

---

## 💡 5. Pro Tips & Troubleshooting

- **Hard Refresh**: If you don't see your changes or the latest editor features, press **`Ctrl + Shift + R`** (Windows) or **`Cmd + Shift + R`** (Mac).
- **Multi-Edit**: You can edit multiple pages and multiple items before clicking "Publish".
- **Safety**: If you make a mistake, simply refresh the page *without* clicking Publish to discard your current session's edits.
