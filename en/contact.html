FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Da'am?
You can volunteer by filling out the volunteer form on the "Get Involved" page, or by visiting our headquarters in Belbeis. We welcome all experiences and fields.
Do you accept in-kind donations?
Yes, we accept in-kind donations such as clothes, medical equipment, and food items to distribute to eligible families after sorting and preparing them.
How do you ensure aid reaches those who deserve it?
We have a specialized field research team that visits families and studies their cases carefully to ensure support reaches the most needy groups according to transparent criteria.
Are there certificates for volunteers?
Yes, we grant accredited experience and volunteer certificates to volunteers who participate effectively in our programs and activities for a specified period.
