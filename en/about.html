Our Story

From a Youth Idea to a Sustainable Institution

Da'am Foundation for Sustainable Development is an Egyptian non-profit organization, registered under No. 3924 of 2025. We were founded by a group of passionate youth who believed in the power of collective action to change reality for the better.

We work on designing and implementing development programs that address the root causes of economic, social, and environmental challenges. Our approach relies on Participatory Development, where we involve the local community in every step of planning and implementation.

  • Only development entity in Belbeis focusing on sustainability.
  • Youth leadership with a modern vision.
  • Strategic partnerships with the private and government sectors.

Our Vision

To be the leading model in achieving comprehensive local sustainable development in Egypt, creating an empowered society capable of managing its resources and shaping its future.

Our Mission

Empowering the most needy groups through innovative programs in economic empowerment, education, and environmental protection, while consolidating the values of volunteerism and social responsibility.
