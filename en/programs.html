Our Programs

The pillars of sustainable development we work on to build a better future.

First Program

Economic Empowerment & Livelihood Improvement

We work on empowering individuals economically to achieve financial independence. This program aims to provide technical and financial support to the most needy groups.

  • Entrepreneurship & Small Businesses: Training and mentoring to support emerging ideas and projects.
  • Financial Inclusion & Job Qualification: Providing the necessary knowledge and skills to integrate into the formal economy.
  • Supporting Handicrafts & Heritage: Preserving cultural heritage and promoting it as a source of income.
  • Supporting Green Economy Programs: Encouraging economic practices that achieve environmental balance.

Second Program

Social & Cultural Development

We strive to enhance social solidarity and cultural expression. This program aims to provide necessary support to groups most in need of care.

  • Supporting Education Programs: Providing high-quality educational opportunities for children and youth.
  • Health & Population Enhancement: Implementing initiatives to raise health awareness and improve services.
  • Social Solidarity & Protection Programs: Providing safety nets for vulnerable groups.
  • Supporting Cultural Activities: Developing talents and expanding horizons of cultural expression.

Third Program

Environmental Development & Climate Change

We address environmental challenges and promote sustainability. We are committed to addressing urgent environmental issues through sustainable initiatives and projects.

  • Climate Change Mitigation: Developing innovative solutions to reduce carbon emissions.
  • Combating Environmental Pollution: Implementing campaigns and projects to reduce pollution in all its forms.
  • Waste Management & Recycling: Developing effective systems for waste management and encouraging recycling.
  • Sustainable Use of Natural Resources: Promoting the rational use of water and energy.
